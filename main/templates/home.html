Tutorial - How to use advAIse to explore your documents?

Start by selecting the documents you want to explore. You can choose from a variety of document types, such as contracts, financial reports, regulations, and much more.

Step 2: Ask your questions

Once you have selected your documents, ask your questions in the search bar. You can ask them in the language of your choice.
